KIUMA has been a blessing in my university journey. The weekly teachings have deepened my understanding of Islam, and the community support has been incredible. I've made lifelong friends here.

— Ahmed Hassan, Year 3 Medical Student

— Omar Ibrahim, Year 4 Business Student

KIUMA provided me with the spiritual guidance I needed during my studies. The mentorship program helped me balance my academic and spiritual life, and I'm forever grateful.

— Yusuf Abdullah, Year 3 Law Student
