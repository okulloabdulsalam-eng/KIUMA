About KIUMA

Our History

KIUMA (Kampala International University Muslim Association) was established to serve the growing Muslim student population at Kampala International University. Since its inception, the association has been dedicated to providing a supportive Islamic environment for students to grow in faith, knowledge, and character.

Over the years, KIUMA has organized numerous educational programs, community service activities, and spiritual gatherings that have positively impacted the lives of countless students. Our commitment to authentic Islamic teachings and community building continues to guide our mission.
