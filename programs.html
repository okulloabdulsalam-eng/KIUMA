Programs & Courses

Comprehensive Islamic Education Programs

Our Educational Programs

Join us in seeking authentic Islamic knowledge

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive Islamic studies covering the fundamentals of faith, worship, and Islamic jurisprudence.

  • Foundations of Islamic faith (Aqeedah)
  • Islamic history and civilization
  • Contemporary Islamic issues
  • Comparative religion studies

Quran Memorization

Structured program for memorizing the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed and understanding.

  • Hifz (Memorization) classes
  • Tajweed (Proper recitation)
  • Quranic Arabic
  • Revision and retention support

Hadith Studies

Study of the authentic sayings and actions of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

  • 40 Nawawi Hadith
  • Riyadus Salihin
  • Hadith methodology
  • Understanding and application

Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence)

Understanding Islamic law and jurisprudence based on authentic sources.

  • Fiqh of worship (Ibadah)
  • Fiqh of transactions (Muamalat)
  • Fiqh of family matters
  • Contemporary Fiqh issues

Tafsir (Quranic Exegesis)

In-depth study and explanation of the meanings of the Holy Quran.

  • Systematic Tafsir study
  • Understanding Quranic themes
  • Context and background
  • Practical application

Student Mentorship

One-on-one and group mentorship programs to support students in their Islamic journey.

  • Personal spiritual guidance
  • Academic support
  • Character development
  • Leadership training
