How to Join KIUMA

Become part of our Islamic community

Requirements

To join KIUMA, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • ✓ Student Status: Must be a current student at Kampala International University
  • ✓ Islamic Faith: Must be a Muslim or interested in learning about Islam
  • ✓ Commitment: Willingness to participate in association activities and programs
  • ✓ Good Character: Commitment to upholding Islamic values and good conduct

Application Process

Step 1: Fill Out the Registration Form

Complete the online registration form with your personal information, contact details, and reasons for joining KIUMA.

Step 2: Submit Your Application

Submit your completed form through our online portal. You will receive a confirmation email upon submission.

Step 3: Review Process

Our leadership team will review your application. This process typically takes 3-5 business days.

Step 4: Welcome & Orientation

Once approved, you will receive a welcome email with details about orientation sessions and upcoming activities.

Ready to Join?

Start your journey with KIUMA today. Fill out the registration form and become part of our vibrant Islamic community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership in KIUMA is free for all eligible students. However, voluntary contributions for activities and charity are welcome.

Do I need prior Islamic knowledge?

No, KIUMA welcomes students at all levels of Islamic knowledge. Our programs are designed to accommodate beginners and advanced learners.

What are the time commitments?

Participation is flexible. You can attend activities and programs based on your schedule. We encourage regular participation but understand academic commitments.

Can I join specific programs only?

Yes, you can participate in specific programs that interest you. However, we encourage full membership to benefit from all activities and community support.
